Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

Browse our catalog, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, GCash, and bank transfers.

Can I track my order status?

You can track your order by navigating to the "Transaction History" in the profile section.

Return Policy

If you receive a defective or incorrect item, contact us within 7 days for a replacement or refund.

To initiate a return, please email us at returns@bastebookmart.com with your order number and photos of the item.

Return shipping costs may be covered depending on the nature of the return.
